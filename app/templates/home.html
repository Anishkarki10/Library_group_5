About Library Management

Your Gateway to Knowledge

A modern library system built to help students discover books, reserve resources, read e-books, and manage their learning journey easily.

Who We Are

A Smarter Way to Manage Library Resources

Library Management is designed to make library access simple for students and easy for administrators. Students can search physical books, check availability, reserve books, view reading history, and access uploaded PDF e-books from one clean portal.

Our goal is to combine the value of traditional libraries with the convenience of modern digital learning.

What Students Can Do

Why It Matters

Simple for Students, Powerful for Admins

Easy Access

Students do not need to manually ask for every book. They can check availability instantly.

Time Saving

Admins can manage books, reservations, e-books, users, returns, and renew requests faster.

Better Tracking

The system keeps records of reservations, returns, cancellations, reading history, and digital resources.
